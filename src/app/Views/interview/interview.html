Tips to Ace Your Interview

1. Focus on your actual experience — share what you did, how you did it, and what you learned. Be specific about your tasks and results.

2. If you don’t have experience yet, be honest. Highlight your willingness to learn, your discipline, and any related training.

3. Read carefully to questions before answering. Keep your answers short but clear.

4. Research basic information about the company and the role so you can align your answers with what they’re looking for.
